About Pulau Weh

Pulau Weh is located in the Andaman Sea and is a small active volcanic island in the northwest of Sumatra, Indonesia, with an area of 156.3 square kilometers. Belongs to a typical tropical climate, hot and humid. The average temperature throughout the year is about 27-28 ‚ÑÉ. The water flow is calm and the visibility is high, making it very suitable for diving.
